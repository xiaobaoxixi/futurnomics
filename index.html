FemaleFuturnomics is a new venture. It is focused on developing meaningful projects among female intra-entrepreneurs, investors and organizations that have a long – lasting impact on society, close the gender gap and make significant contributions to economic growth.

We think we can build a better and more sustainable world for us and for new generations. Our mission is to reinforce women assets and that might be seen like a Feminism, let´s be clear, YES, we are feminist but we strongly believe that female and males are stronger together. Part of our mission is to engage males on our cause, that it is a worthy cause that brings wealth for all.

Therefore, we must strive valiantly, with grit, passion and innovative business models… we are not afraid of fail, we will come again and again, because this is our vision. We are born to uplift, protect and promote women. To shine a light on solutions and move society forward. It is our gift to the world.
